Admission Informations and Requirements

Thank you for your interest in Kibo School of Technology

Here are some important information about our admission process:

To be eligible for admission, you must

  • Be at least 16 years of age
  • Live within 100km (2 hours drive) of a local hub:
    • Nigeria: Abeokuta, Ibadan and Lagos
    • Ghana: Accra and Kumasi
    • Kenya: Nairobi
  • Provide a proof of secondary school graduation
  • Demonstrate English language proficency, and the ability to read, write, speak and understand English to the degree requred to engage in University-level learning

To apply, please visit Kibo School of Technology Official Admissions website for the online application. Kibo Admission

Admission Steps

  1. Attend the Information Session

    2. Attend tthe Information session to meet Kibo team, hear from the current students, and determine if Kibo is a good fit for you.

  2. Complete the Online Application

    3. Complete the online application that gives us more information about yourself, including your educational background and personal interest.

  3. Kibo Challenge

    4. Complete the Kibo Challenge which will you determine your readiness for the online school, and this challenge will also motivate you to study at Kibo.

  4. Reasoning Test

    5. You have to complete the online reasonong test that consists of logical and mathematical reasoning questions.

  5. Finalist Interview

    6. You will receive an email that will inform you if you passed the admission process. There you will schedule a virtual interview.
